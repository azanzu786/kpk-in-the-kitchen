Pumpkin Spice Cookies

  • Ingredient List
  • 2 1/2 cups AP Flour
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted Butter
  • 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Organic Vanilla Extract
  • 1 cup Pumpkin Puree
  • For Frosting: 1 cup Confectioner's Sugar Milk as per desired frosting consistency
