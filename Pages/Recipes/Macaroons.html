Coconut Macaroon

  •     Ingredient List    
  • 400g Sweetened Shredded Coconut
  • 300mL Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • 1 Tsp Organic Vanilla Extract (non-alcoholic)
  • 2 Large Egg Whites (leave at room temperature)
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
