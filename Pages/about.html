ABOUT US

If there is one thing that brings people together, regardless of culture and language, it is food! We are a group of ethnic Pashtuns from the mountains of Northern Pakistan who have moved miles away to Canada but still like to remember our roots. Our goal is to connect with the world over our love for food, by creating and testing recipes inspired by our own culture as well as others.
